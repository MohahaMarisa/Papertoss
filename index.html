Hello these are instructions for our game! Your goal is to get the trash into the trash bin. The moving bar to the left of your screen is your power bar which controls how much power the ball is thrown with. The two black dots on the power bar are what your power level should be in order to get the trash in the bin. if aimed conrectly The ball will go in the bin and you will have successfuly completed the level. After winning you can move onto different progressively harder levels!
